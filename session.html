What I deliver

Session services

Lead vocals

In-the-pocket lead takes across pop, soul, dance, house and more, always in service of the song.

Backing & harmonies

Arranged backing stacks, doubles, ad-libs and vocal textures to add width and lift to your production.

Topline & demos

Melodies and hooks written to your instrumental, delivered as a demo you can pitch or build on.

Remote sessions

Send stems or a rough; I'll track in my own space and return dry, tuned and comped stems, ready to mix.

In-studio

Prefer to be in the room? I'm happy to track at your studio and take direction live on the session.

Delivery you can trust

Labelled files, consistent gain, and versions on request. A tidy handover every time.
