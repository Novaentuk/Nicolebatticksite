My story

From singing along to leading the band

Music has never been a hobby for me — it's been the thread running through everything. I started out singing anywhere that would have me, learning fast that a great performance isn't about the biggest notes; it's about reading a room and giving people a moment they'll remember.

Across more than a thousand events and sessions over the past decade, I've grown from a singer into a music entrepreneur. I founded Nova Entertainment to give couples and event planners live bands they can genuinely rely on, and I've built a career alongside it in the studio — lending vocals to other people's records, writing songs, and coaching the next wave of singers.

Whatever hat I'm wearing — bandleader, session vocalist, writer or coach — the goal is the same: bring warmth, professionalism and a little bit of magic to every project I touch.
