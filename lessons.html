Who it's for

Wherever you're starting from

Complete beginners

Never had a lesson? Perfect. We'll build healthy foundations and get you singing songs you love from day one.

Improvers

Already sing but hit a ceiling? We'll expand your range, control and tone, and iron out the habits holding you back.

Performers & pros

Gigging singers wanting stamina, consistency and stagecraft — plus set prep and audition coaching when you need it.

What we'll cover

Technique that actually sticks

  • Breath support, posture and healthy vocal habits
  • Range, power, control and tone
  • Pitch, timing and ear training
  • Style, phrasing and making a song your own
  • Stage presence, nerves and performance confidence
  • Audition, set and recording preparation
